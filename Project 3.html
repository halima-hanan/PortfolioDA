Project Overview

Background

RevoGrocers is a fictional grocery retail business operating in multiple locations with a diverse product range. The project analyzes sales performance using a Kaggle dataset to enhance data-driven decision-making. It specifically investigates how different product categories and customer behaviors influence overall company revenue.

Method

  • data cleaning and transformation of Sales, Product, and Category tables using SQL in Google BigQuery
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to identify top-performing product categories and analyze the primary factors driving revenue. Additionally, it evaluates pricing strategies by assessing the relationship between average unit prices and customer demand across various categories.

Result

  • Confections emerged as the top category, contributing 12.87% of revenue with a 99.85% repeat purchase rate
  • Insights show revenue is driven by unit price and volume rather than customer count
  • recommendations to optimize inventory for top categories and prioritize high-value customer retention
