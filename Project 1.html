Scope of Work

  • Analyzed revenue trends from 2012 to 2015, identifying an abnormal 10% quarter-over-quarter decline in Q1 2015
  • Isolated the root cause of revenue loss to a session decline, specifically a 75% drop in non-brand campaigns.
  • Validated platform performance by confirming average order value and conversion rates remained stable through the Q1 period
  • Developed a prioritized recovery plan focusing on non-brand desktop search acquisition and structural traffic monitoring

Project Overview

Background

Maven Fuzzy Factory, an e-commerce business, faced a sharp revenue drop in Q1 2015 after consistent growth. This project performs a root cause analysis to determine why revenue fell by 10% quarter-over-quarter.

Method

  • business understanding
  • data cleaning
  • exploratory data analysis and visualization to test hypotheses regarding AOV, conversion rates, and engagement
  • deep dive into session traffic by device and channel was conducted to isolate the root cause

Result

  • Revenue declined due to fewer transactions driven by a structural loss in desktop traffic, primarily from non-brand gsearch campaigns which accounted for over 75% of the session drop.
  • Recommendations include prioritizing the recovery of non-brand desktop acquisition, reallocating budget toward high-impact channels, and implementing early-warning monitoring to detect future search traffic declines before they impact revenue
